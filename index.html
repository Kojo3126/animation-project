A.R.T.

Aspire. Reinvent. Transform.

The A.R.T. initiative aims to both inform and inspire budding artists. We have a varied selection of different kinds of design, creating a gallery ensured to influence users. But what makes our site unique is the randomness of the images generated. Going into a project with a certain direction may not always be the right decision. This is an opportunity for all users to become motivated, perhaps in ways they aren't expecting.

    Some Useful Tips for Navigation:
  1. The "Color Mode" button generates random background colors to give the users ideas for their own artwork.
  2. The "Night Mode" button is particularly useful for those who want to explore late into the night without the glare of the screen distracting them.
  3. The remaining four buttons show four categories of art disciplines. The user can click on each one to gain insight.
