A.R.T: Aspire, Reinvent, Transform

Our initiative aims to both inform and inspire budding artists. We have a varied selection of different kinds of design, creating a gallery ensured to influence users. But what makes our site unique is the randomness of the images generated. Going into a project with a certain direction may not always be the right decision. This is a chance for all users to becoming motivated, perhaps in ways they aren't expecting.

  1. The "Color Mode" button generates random background colors to give the users ideas for their own artwork.
  2. The "Night Mode" button is particularly useful for those who want to work late into the night without the glare of the screen distracting them.
